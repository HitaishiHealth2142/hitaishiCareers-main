Frequently Asked Questions

For Job Seekers

To apply for a job, you first need to create an account and complete your profile. Once logged in, browse our job listings, click on a job that interests you, and hit the "Apply Now" button to submit your application directly to the employer.

You can update your resume by navigating to your User Profile and selecting the "Resume" tab. You can either use our built-in Resume Builder tool or upload a new PDF/Word document directly.

Yes! Creating a profile, searching for jobs, and applying to positions is completely free for all job seekers.

You can find experienced mentors in your field through our Mentorships page. You can request a session, ask for career guidance, and get personalized advice to accelerate your career growth.

For Employers

Employers need to register an account. After verification, log in to your Employer Dashboard, click on "Post a Job," and fill in the necessary details such as job title, skills required, salary, and location.

All applications are tracked in your Employer Dashboard. Go to the "Manage Applications" section to review candidate profiles, resumes, and change selection statuses.
