How can we help you today?

Select a category below to find solutions to your problems.

Job Application Problems

First, ensure your profile is 100% complete. Many jobs require a resume and complete educational details before you can apply. If your profile is complete, try logging out and logging back in, or clear your browser cache.

Go to your User Profile and navigate to the "Applied Jobs" section. Any updates from the employer (e.g., Shortlisted, Rejected, Interview Scheduled) will be updated there.

Currently, applications submitted directly to employers cannot be withdrawn. However, you can update your profile which reflects automatically to employers reviewing your application.

Profile & Resume Problems

Please ensure the image file is in JPG or PNG format and the size is below 5MB. If the problem persists, try using a different browser.

Navigate to your User Profile and click on the "Resume" tab. You can either use the Resume Builder to generate a new one, or upload your existing document (PDF/Docx) directly.

Go to the Login page and click on "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address to receive a password reset link.

Job Posting Problems

Newly posted jobs may take up to 24 hours to be reviewed by our moderation team to prevent spam. Once approved, it will be visible to all candidates.

Log in to your Employer Dashboard, go to "Manage Jobs", and click the Three Dots icon next to the job. You will find options to "Edit", "Deactivate", or "Close" the listing.

Candidate contact details are only fully revealed if you shortlist the candidate or unlock their profile through your subscription plan. Check your Current Plan details under the billing section.

General Queries

Yes, registering, creating a profile, searching for jobs, and applying to positions is 100% free for job seekers.

You can find experienced mentors in your field through our Mentorships page. You can request a session, ask for career guidance, and get personalized advice to accelerate your career growth.
