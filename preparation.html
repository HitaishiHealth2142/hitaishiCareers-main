Interview Preparation Tips for Freshers

📋

Research the Company

Study the company's mission, values, recent news, and culture before the interview to demonstrate genuine interest.

🎯

Prepare Your Stories

Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to answer behavioral questions effectively with concrete examples.

👔

Dress Professionally

First impressions matter. Wear appropriate attire that matches the company's dress code and culture expectations.

💬

Practice Communication

Speak clearly, maintain good eye contact, and avoid filler words like "um" and "uh" by practicing beforehand.
