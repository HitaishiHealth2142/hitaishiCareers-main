Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Career Booster

Is Career Booster guaranteed to get me a job?

While we can't guarantee employment (that depends on your qualifications and interview performance), Career Booster significantly increases your visibility and chances. On average, users receive 3-5x more interview requests compared to normal profiles.

Can I cancel my Career Booster anytime?

Yes! You can cancel anytime through your account settings. If you cancel during an active period, you'll retain access until the end of your current billing cycle.

How quickly will I see results?

Most users see increased profile views within 24-48 hours. The exact timing depends on when recruiters in your field are actively searching. We recommend keeping Career Booster active for at least 2 weeks for optimal results.

Do I need to update my profile separately?

Career Booster enhances your existing profile. For best results, we recommend ensuring your profile is complete before activating. Our professional resume rewrite service (included in monthly+ plans) will help optimize your content.

Is there a free trial available?

We don't offer free trials, but we have a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the results. Just contact our support team within 7 days of purchase.
